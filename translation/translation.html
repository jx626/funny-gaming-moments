1. The Science of Masculinity

Rival Knowledges

The concepts 'masculine' and 'feminine', Freud observed in a melancholy footnote, 'are among the most confused that occur in science'.1 In many practical situations the language of 'mascu­line' and 'feminine' raises few doubts.
